About AR Lithium Report

AR Lithium Report is an independent business-intelligence publication dedicated to lithium: the markets, the companies and the policy decisions shaping the industry — with Argentina and the Lithium Triangle (Argentina, Chile and Bolivia) at the centre of our coverage.

What we cover

  • Markets — lithium prices, equities of producers, and the forces moving them.
  • Companies — projects, financing, production and strategy across the sector.
  • The Triangle — country-by-country coverage of Argentina, Chile and Bolivia.
  • Interviews — conversations with the executives and analysts running the industry.

Our standards

Every article names the publications and primary sources it draws on. Market data is sourced from Yahoo Finance and timestamped. Photography is used under free licenses (NASA, ESA, INPE, Wikimedia Commons) and credited on every image.

AR Lithium Report is edited by J. Martineau. The publication is editorially independent and accepts no payment for coverage.
