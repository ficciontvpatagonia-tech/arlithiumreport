Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 2, 2026

AR Lithium Report is built to be read, not to collect data. This policy explains, in plain language, what happens when you visit this site.

Nothing directly. This site has no user accounts, no comment system, no forms and sets no tracking cookies of its own. We do not collect, store or sell personal information.

Hosting

The site is served by GitHub Pages. As with any web host, GitHub may log technical data such as IP addresses for security and operational purposes. See GitHub's privacy statement.

Embedded content

Some pages embed video players from YouTube. When you play a video, YouTube (Google) may set cookies and process data under Google's privacy policy. Videos do not autoplay; no data is sent to YouTube until you interact with a player.

Market data

Price and equity data displayed on this site is retrieved from public financial sources by our own systems before publication. Your browser does not connect to those sources when you read the site.

External links

Articles may reference other publications. Their privacy practices are their own.

Changes and contact

If our practices change (for example, if we add a newsletter or contact forms), this policy will be updated before those features go live. Questions: contact us.
